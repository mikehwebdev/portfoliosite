Useful links

National Smallbore Rifle Association (NSRA)

The NSRA is the national governing body for all Small-bore Rifle and Pistol Target Shooting in the United Kingdom.

National Rifle Association

The NRA is the national governing body for target shooting with fullbore rifle & pistol in the United Kingdom.

Norfolk County Rifle Association

We are affiliated to the Norfolk County Association and take part in the competitions they run throughtout the year.

Suffolk County Rifle Association

The Suffolk Smallbore Association represents all Suffolk clubs which participate in .22 Target Rifle Shooting.

Norfolk Police Firearms Licensing Department

Responsible for the issue of certificates, permits and advice for firearms, shotguns and explosives in Norfolk.

Suffolk Police Firearms Licensing Department

Responsible for the issue of certificates, permits and advice for firearms, shotguns and explosives in Suffolk.
